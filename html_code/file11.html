Shocking footage: 17 year old rams speeding car into a women

PIC FROM Caters News

A woman has miraculously survived after being dragged along by a speeding joyrider which then smashed into another car.

Kanta Anand, 40, was walking out of her home in Delhi, India, when the 17 year old driver ploughed into her.

She is then dragged more than 50 yards by the white MUV car before horror footage shows it ramming into a black vehicle pulling out of a junction.

PIC FROM Caters News

The CCTV images show Ms Anand being catapulted into the black car as it flips onto its side.

Incredibly she only suffered a fractured hip and following surgery is now stable in hospital.

The driver, who cannot be named for legal reasons, attempted to flee and reverses away but locals grabbed him following the accident on New Years Day.

PIC FROM Caters News

His parents were attending a religious ceremony and had no hint that their son, a school student, had taken the car for the joyride.

The father, who runs a security agency, was fined but the driver was let off as he is only a teenager.

A police spokesman said: “He could not be arrested as he is a juvenile but we have issued an apprehension memo and produced the boy before the Child Welfare Committee. The boy’s father was also issued a fine under the Motor Vehicle Act.
